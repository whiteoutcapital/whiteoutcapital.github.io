# My strategy

I'm a generalist investor and will look almost anywhere if I think I can earn an attractive return.

My investments have spanned global public equities, real estate, crypto, private equity, and more. It's not unusual for me to own a $10 million market cap company and a $100 billion one at the same time. I'll occasionally venture into special situations, biotech, or precious metals if the opportunity is compelling.

With that said, my bread and butter is global microcaps.

I'm usually looking for small companies that can compound per-share value over time. I love finding businesses that don't screen well today but might in a few years. I get even more excited when I share an idea and most people don't like it (yet).
